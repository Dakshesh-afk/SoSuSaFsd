# ?? SIMPLE 5-STEP GUIDE TO SEE YOUR POSTS

## Problem
You can't see categories and posts even though seeding is implemented.

## Why
**The home feed ONLY shows posts from categories you follow!**

## Solution in 5 Simple Steps

### Step 1: Open PowerShell
Press `Windows + X`, then click "PowerShell" or "Terminal"

### Step 2: Run These 4 Commands (Copy & Paste)
```powershell
cd C:\Users\louis\source\repos\SoSuSaFsdd\SoSuSaFsd
dotnet ef database drop --force
dotnet ef database update
dotnet run
```


### Step 3: Open Browser
Go to: `https://localhost:5001`

### Step 4: Login
- Email: `louis@example.com`
- Password: `User@123`

### Step 5: Follow a Category
1. Look at the **RIGHT sidebar** (you should see "Verified Categories")
2. Click on **#SG News** or **#Temasek Poly News** (or any category)
3. Click the **"Follow"** button
4. Click **"Home"** in the navigation
5. **You should now see posts!** ??
